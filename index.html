Societal Impact Resource Finder

Whether applying for grants, academic tenure or job promotion you will be required to demonstrate the societal impact of your research. These impacts could be social, cultural, health, environmental or economic.

Have you contributed to improved services, healthier lives, better welfare, as well as increased understanding of ideas and attitudes, values and beliefs? It is important to capture this information.
This tool will help to supplement your academic metrics and altmetrics to build your impact story.

TIP: Log your research impact and engagement activities. This will help you gather evidence to support your claims.
A great tool for this purpose is VV Impact Tracker.
